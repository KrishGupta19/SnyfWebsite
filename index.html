The Problem

Today's Review Platforms Are Broken

Current review systems fail both consumers and businesses in critical ways.

🤖

Fake Reviews

Bots and paid reviews flood platforms, making it nearly impossible to know what's real. Trust is eroding fast.

⭐

Shallow Star Ratings

A single number tells you nothing. Is a 3-star restaurant bad overall, or just mediocre on delivery but excellent dine-in?

📉

Zero Business Insights

Businesses get vanity metrics, not actionable data. They can't understand why people love or leave them.

Our Solution

AI That Turns Noise Into Signal

Snyf doesn't just aggregate stars — it structures every review into meaningful dimensions: food, ambience, service, value, and more. Our AI then synthesizes thousands of opinions into one clear, honest verdict.

  • ✓ Structured multi-dimensional review forms
  • ✓ AI-generated natural language summaries per category
  • ✓ Real-time sentiment tracking over time
  • ✓ Verified reviewer system to eliminate bots

Features

Everything You Need to Trust Local

01

Structured Reviews

Reviewers answer targeted dimension-based questions, not just a star rating. Every review contributes meaningful, comparable data.

02

AI-Generated Summaries

Our models read hundreds of reviews and produce concise, honest summaries — no more reading through pages of contradictory opinions.

03

Sentiment Insights

Track how sentiment evolves over time. Did the new chef improve food quality? Did a renovation hurt ambience? Know immediately.

Businesses get a private analytics dashboard showing their strengths, weaknesses, and actionable steps to improve their local standing.

Find the best spots near you filtered by real dimensions that matter — not just proximity and an average star that ignores context.

Our anti-fraud layer ensures only real, verified visitors leave reviews — eliminating bots, paid shills, and competitor sabotage.

About Us

Built by People Who Love Local

We're a team of engineers, designers, and data scientists who got tired of being misled by star ratings. We've experienced the frustration of showing up to a 4-star restaurant that turned out to be mediocre — because 200 fake reviews inflated its score.

Snyf was born from that frustration. We believe local businesses deserve honest, structured feedback — and that customers deserve to make informed decisions based on what actually matters to them.

We're building in public, iterating fast, and listening to our early community every step of the way.
