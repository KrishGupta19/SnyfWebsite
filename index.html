Founding Principles

The Charter
Behind the Standard

Snyf was founded by engineers, researchers, and local commerce advocates who identified a fundamental market failure: the complete collapse of review system integrity.

Our institutional framework is built on three non-negotiable axioms. These are not marketing values — they are operational constraints that govern every product decision we make.

A

Accuracy Over Engagement

Every architectural decision prioritizes data integrity above platform metrics. We do not optimize for time-on-site.

C

Community Sovereignty

The review index belongs to the community that builds it. We are the infrastructure, not the authority.

L

Local-First Mandate

Local commerce is the backbone of community. Protecting it from informational exploitation is our core mandate.
